How it works: ____________________________ On the begin you have a menu with 3 buttons. The buttons are: Play, Help and Quit. When you press the play button the game starts. When you press the Help button you get information about the game. And when you press the quit button the program will end. If you play the game the main objectif is to get 1000 points. You need to kill 100 enemys to gain that amount of score. And you need to watch out for the enemys because they will hunt you. And if they touch you, you will get damage. If one enemey touch you and he dealt damage, he will turns pink and wil freeze 15 secondes. And if you kill one enemey he will turn with and he will fall on the ground. If you die you will get a dead screen with a restart button and a main menu button. The restart button will restart the game. And the main menu button will take you back to the menu. And if you manage to get the amount of points you will get a victory screen with two buttons. Restart button and quit button. Beanwars Made with: I made this game in unity. I used the code C#. And I used the program Visual Studio 2019.
