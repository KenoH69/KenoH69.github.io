Beanwars Pong 10 level Parkour
How it works: On the begin of the game you have the main screen. On this screen you see two buttons, the Play button and the Quit button. If you press the play buttone the game will start. And if you press the quit button the game will close. If you play the game you start on level 1 and the main objectif is to get to level 10. On level 1 you can't jump and need to dodge red cubes. If you fall in the void you will respawn on level 1. And if you completed level 1 you will actived a checkpoint. In level 2 you need to pass by a path with moving cubes. On level 3-7 is just parkour and you need to dodge or jump on some objects .On level 8 you will get a jump boost if you jump on the green platforms. On level 9 you need to choose the correct path. And on the final level you get a speedboost and need to manage to get to the finish line. If you reached the finsih line you will get a victory screen. On the screen you have two buttons restart and quit. If you press restart it will play the game again, and quit will close the game. Also you can press ESC and you will go to the main menu. 10 level parkour Made with: I made this game in unity. I used the code C#. And I used the program Visual Studio 2019.
